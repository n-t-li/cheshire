Health and Safety

At Cheshire Reach and Wash, Health and Safety is more than just a matter of simply complying with rules and regulations - it is the core principle of how we act out and live our everyday working lives.

We constantly strive to maintain the highest health and safety standards, not only for ourselves, but also for others by being proactive, not reactive.

It is by the active engagement of an entire team commitment at Cheshire Reach and Wash, which leads directly to maintaining improved safety in the workplace.

The current legal working height for ladders to be used to clean your windows is 29 feet. Any windows that you have over that height must be cleaned by using another method such as the reach and wash system.

Any windows over 18 feet and under 29 feet cannot be cleaned using ladders without the use of ladder ties or secondary personnel support, in accordance with Health & Safety regulations.

We consult with our employees on matters affecting their health and safety, ensure that they are fully competent to do their tasks, provide adequate accident prevention training and safe and healthy working conditions.

We are fully insured, We also accept our responsibility for the Health and Safety of our customers and other people who may be affected by our activities.
