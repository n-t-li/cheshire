About Us

Cheshire Reach and Wash is a family owned business operating in and around Cheshire for over 14 years.

We are a fully insured professional window cleaning company and look after a wealth of customers in Sandiway, Cuddington, Delamere, Tarporley, Tarvin and Cheshire.

OUR MISSION

We aim to become the number one trusted provider of window cleaning services in Cheshire, the North West and surrounding area. We will achieve this by offering a consistently high standard of work and staff. We will strive for perfection in all that we do and deliver 100% customer satisfaction.

Our single largest source of business is word of mouth, this is not a coincidence, we will only employ staff who are experts in their field, nice people to deal with, respectful, reliable, and punctual.

We guarantee every window washed and welcome your feedback. If a mistake is made we will do everything we can to correct the situation. Be this a re-wash on the same day or at your convenience or your money back.
