Commercial Window Cleaner in Cheshire

Cheshire Reach and Wash have developed a highly skilled, thorough and efficient commercial window cleaning operation to deliver a variety of solutions for every type of business premises, from private to public workplace including company, commercial, office, corporate office, councils, and retail.

We offer our customers a reliable, safe and cost effective solution combining both traditional window cleaning skills and trained use of state-of-the art window cleaning technologies.

Latest technology

We use the ‘Reach & Wash’ – a purified water system - the latest technology in window cleaning. Please watch the video below for more information.

Our vehicle fleet each contain a dedicated holding tank, which supply a lightweight telescopic pole with soft brush attachment. Without the requirement of added chemicals or ladders, we are able to access and clean windows to heights in excess of 65 feet - or 5-6 floors - to achieve the best possible results.
